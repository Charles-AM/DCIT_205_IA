What is Computer Science?

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems. It is a broad and diverse field, encompassing everything from the design of hardware and software to the development of algorithms and artificial intelligence. Computer scientists are essential for solving the complex problems of the modern world.

Why Study Computer Science at University Of Ghana?

  • We offer a world-class education in computer science, with a curriculum that is designed to prepare you for a successful career in the tech industry.
  • Our faculty are passionate about teaching and research, and they are dedicated to helping you succeed.
  • Our students have access to state-of-the-art facilities, including cutting-edge research labs and high-performance computing resources.
  • We offer a variety of student organizations and activities, including a programming club, a robotics club, and a cybersecurity club.
  • Our graduates are highly sought-after by employers in the tech industry, and they command top salaries.

What Can I Do with a Degree in Computer Science?

A degree in computer science can open doors to a wide range of exciting and rewarding careers. Some of the most popular careers for computer scientists include:

  • Software engineer
  • Web developer
  • Database administrator
  • Network administrator
  • Information security analyst
  • Data scientist
  • Machine learning engineer
  • Artificial intelligence researcher
